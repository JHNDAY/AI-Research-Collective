The Bigger Picture

OpenAI's aggressive pursuit of media partnerships represents a strategic effort to legitimize AI-generated content while addressing copyright concerns. By forming licensing deals, OpenAI aims to create a sustainable model that compensates content creators while expanding its training data and user experience capabilities.
