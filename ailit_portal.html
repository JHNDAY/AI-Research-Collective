The AILit Mission

Learning by Doing

Every session includes a hands-on element — working with AI tools directly, not just discussing them from a distance.

Critical Engagement

We ask hard questions about AI claims, errors, and epistemology — applying the same standards we use for any knowledge source.

Immersive Reading

Close reading with AI as an active interlocutor — from Shakespeare to news analysis — is a core SIG practice.

Structured Dialogue

The Ed Murrow protocol — developed in the AILit course — shapes our discussions: deliberate, evidence-grounded, multiple voices.

Building Fluency

Moving BOLLI members from passive observers to confident, purposeful users of AI tools — the core goal of the SIG.

Double Resonance

AILit carries two meanings at once: AI Literacy, and the pre-digital lit of the 1960s — a word that still means something.
