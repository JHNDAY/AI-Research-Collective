Choose Your Topic

Twenty-one starting points for the Narrative Prompt Coach — pulled from what this cohort already cares about, spanning everything from quick answers to genuine thinking partnership.

You don't need to know AI to start. You just need a topic that's actually yours.

Below are seven areas drawn from what members of this group told us they're curious about, working on, or wrestling with. Each area has three doors in — a gentle one, a more involved one, and one for whoever's ready to push further. Pick whichever calls to you. There's no wrong door.

One thing to keep in mind: whatever you write becomes part of what's shared back with John and, in aggregate, with the rest of the cohort. A general question works just as well as a deeply personal one — keep specifics to whatever you'd be comfortable having others see.

1. Find a topic below that fits something real in your life.
2. Open the Narrative Prompt Coach and write your first draft — rough is fine.
3. Revise with Claude's feedback, then generate your response and your prompt-evolution visualization.
4. Send both to John — they'll become part of our cohort-wide Grand Learning View.
