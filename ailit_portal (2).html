About AILit
Our Purpose
The AI Lit Mission

From English Lit to AI Lit, the word has evolved but carries forward — still meaning something fundamentally human.

🧠 AI Concepts

Understanding how AI works: what it is, how it thinks, its real strengths, and its genuine limitations.

💻 Digital Fluency

Navigating browser interfaces with ease and confidence — the foundation everything else rests on.

💬 Conversational Skill

Learning to dialogue with AI iteratively — prompting well, following threads, and building deeper understanding.

Learn by Doing

Every session includes a hands-on element — working with AI tools directly, not just discussing them from a distance.

Immersive Reading

Close reading with AI as an active interlocutor — from Shakespeare to news analysis — is a core SIG practice.

Balance Organic and Digital Intelligence

AI accelerates the work, but judgment, curiosity, and interpretation remain irreducibly human — the liberal arts as the mind's own intelligence.
