The Key Distinction · Ed Murrow

Ethics governs conduct — right and wrong action.
Justice governs distribution — who gets what, and why.
They are not the same thing. Most of what we call "unjust" AI use is actually an ethical breach. True injustice is about access, power, and who is excluded.
